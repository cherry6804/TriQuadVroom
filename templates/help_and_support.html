Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I post a ride? - Navigate to the 'Post Ride' section, fill in the ride details, and click 'Submit'.
  • How do I request a ride? - Use the search function to find available rides, then click 'Request' to notify the driver.
  • How can I track my ride status? - Visit your ride history to view the status of past and upcoming rides.
